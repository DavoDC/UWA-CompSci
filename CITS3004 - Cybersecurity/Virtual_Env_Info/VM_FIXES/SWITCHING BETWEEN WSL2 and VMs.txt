ENABLE VIRTUAL MACHINES AND DISABLE WSL2:
(DISABLES DOCKER AS WELL) 

bcdedit /set hypervisorlaunchtype off

RESTART!!!
WSL will give error , you cannot access files
VM will no longer say credential guard error or others




ENABLE WSL2 + DOCKER AND DISABLE VIRTUAL MACHINES:

bcdedit /set hypervisorlaunchtype auto

RESTART!!!
WSL will work and you can access files
VM will say credential guard error or others
